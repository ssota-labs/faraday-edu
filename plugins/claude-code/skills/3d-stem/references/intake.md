# Intake — clarify the STEM concept

Before scaffolding, pin the few facts that shape the lesson.

## Ask (or infer and confirm)

1. **Topic** — the concept in one sentence.
2. **Learner level** — middle school · secondary · undergrad · professional · general.
3. **Success click** — what should feel obvious after manipulating the scene?
4. **Constraints** — time, prior knowledge, must-include / must-avoid.
5. **Source materials** (optional) — notes, slides, textbook excerpt.

## Suitability gate

Proceed only if the idea is clearer when **seen and changed in 3D** (spatial,
geometric, mechanical, field, graph-embedded-in-space, algorithm-as-structure).

If not suitable, reframe (“show the spatial intuition behind X”) or refuse with
a short reason. Do not force a 3D wrapper onto a purely symbolic drill.

# third-law — Newton's 3rd law: action–reaction

- **outcome:** The learner can identify the action–reaction pair (equal magnitude,
  opposite direction, on DIFFERENT bodies) and explain why equal forces give unequal
  accelerations for unequal masses (recognize/distinguish verb).
- **interaction:** (1) ConcepTest pretest — truck vs car collision: which force is bigger?
  (2) Model — two skaters/carts of adjustable mass ratio push off (`useRafLoop`); equal &
  opposite forces, but a = F/m so the lighter one recoils faster; live velocity Readouts
  show mₐvₐ = m_b v_b. (3) `<Chart>` recoil speed vs mass (∝1/m) tying back to 2nd law.
- **check:** `<Quiz>` ConcepTest — big truck hits small car: forces are equal & opposite
  (distractors = "truck exerts more", "they cancel"). Re-quiz second-law (spacing).
- **source:** Newton III; pair forces on different bodies; momentum split. **packs:** map2d.

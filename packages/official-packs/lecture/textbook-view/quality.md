# Pack `textbook-view` — quality bar

- **Denser than slide view.** Pages carry paragraphs and worked steps — not
  one-line captions.
- **Still interactive.** Charts, quizzes, and manipulables belong inside pages;
  textbook view is not a static PDF.
- **Paired intent.** When slide view exists for the same lecture, textbook pages
  expand the same beats — don't fork into a different topic.
- **Free mode notes.** `notesKey` is stable; margin notes persist per page id.

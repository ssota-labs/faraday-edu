# Pack `slide-view` — quality bar

- **One beat per slide.** Each screen carries a single claim/reveal/question/
  interaction; no slide is a paragraph or two ideas fighting for space.
- **Motion reveals.** Every animation builds, transitions, or focuses — nothing
  spins/bounces for flair; `prefers-reduced-motion` degrades cleanly.
- **Still interactive.** Slide view isn't read-only — the learner manipulates and
  predicts across the slides, not just clicks "next".
- **Right format.** Slide view is chosen because the content wants deliberate,
  one-idea-per-screen pacing — not to dress up dense reference material.
- **Self-contained slides.** Because per-slide state resets, each slide makes sense
  on its own when revisited.

# Pack `assets-3d` — quality bar

- **glTF/GLB preferred.** Single-file GLB for lesson `public/models/`.
- **License clear.** CC0 or explicit grant; Poly Haven / Khronos samples are safe defaults.
- **Procedural-first.** Use `three` pack helpers before importing heavy meshes.
- **Performance.** Under ~5 MB per model for web; compress textures; avoid 4K on mobile.
- **2D fallback.** When 3D is overkill, render to sprite sheet (see `assets-2d`).

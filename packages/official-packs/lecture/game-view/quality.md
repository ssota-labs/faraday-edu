# Pack `game-view` — quality bar

- **Not slides.** Beats are scenes and dialogue, not one-idea-per-screen decks.
- **Movement matters.** At least one `move` beat per scene when a character is present.
- **Short dialogue.** Preschool: ~1 sentence per line, read-aloud friendly; under ~12 words.
- **Big tap targets.** `choice` buttons use large touch areas; no tiny UI.
- **CRA for teaching.** Concrete interaction beat before symbols; celebrate on success.
- **Assets licensed.** Sprites/backgrounds are CC0 or author-owned — see `assets-2d`.

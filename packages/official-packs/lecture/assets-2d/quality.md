# Pack `assets-2d` — quality bar

- **License clear.** CC0, author-owned, or explicit commercial grant — never "found on Google".
- **Consistent style.** One art direction per lecture (pixel, flat vector, hand-drawn) — don't mix.
- **Sized for touch.** Preschool sprites readable at ~26% stage width; backgrounds not too busy.
- **Paths stable.** Use `public/assets/sprites/` and `public/assets/backgrounds/` — no hotlinking.
- **Fallback OK.** Inline SVG placeholders are fine during authoring; swap before ship.

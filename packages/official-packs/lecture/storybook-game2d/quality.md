# Pack `storybook-game2d` — quality bar

Inherits `game2d` quality, plus:

- **CRA order.** Concrete (tap/drag in the Pixi page) → picture → symbol last and
  small. Never open a storybook page on bare notation.
- **One idea per page.** `StorybookGame` pages, not a scroll wall; under ~50 words
  of narration per page when aimed at early readers.
- **Finger-sized targets.** Large hit areas inside the stage; drag/tap over typing.
- **Missions, not definitions.** Checks are "do it" moments on a page (or a
  Faraday `<Quiz>` with picture options after the page).
- **Celebrated.** Page-clear and story-end use audio/XP/burst — success is felt.
- **Story serves learning.** Plot dressing is fine; every page still advances an
  idea or checks one.

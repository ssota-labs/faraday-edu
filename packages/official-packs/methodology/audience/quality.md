# Pack `audience` — quality bar

- **Audience pinned.** The lesson states who the learner is (or the flagged
  assumption). Building for "everyone" is building for no one.
- **One methodology.** A single audience-appropriate method leads (CRA / 5E / Peer
  Instruction / Mayer / Merrill) — not a blend of all five on every node.
- **Register + density match.** Prose register, symbol density, and which
  interaction carries the load fit the population — not a one-size delivery.
- **Layout fits.** The reading surface (scroll / paged / course / world) follows
  the audience default unless the content or creator overrides it.
- **Not learning-styles.** Method adapts to prior knowledge + motivation, never to
  a claimed visual/auditory "style".

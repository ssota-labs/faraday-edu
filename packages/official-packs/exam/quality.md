# Pack `exam` — quality bar

- **Blueprint-traceable.** Every item maps to a stated outcome with a weight; no
  orphan items, and the mix matches the table of specifications (not all recall).
- **Honest distractors.** Each MCQ wrong option is a nameable misconception, not
  filler; numeric items state units + tolerance.
- **Review that teaches.** The exam ends in a per-outcome result + item review that
  names the misconception and points back into the lesson — not a bare score.
- **Mastery, not curve.** Pass is a criterion (threshold + no outcome floored),
  and the learner can retry missed outcomes.
- **Fair.** No answer/hint leaks during the exam; option/item randomization keeps
  every attempt blueprint-equivalent; timing (if any) is stated and accessible.

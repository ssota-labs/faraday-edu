# Pack `kids` — quality bar

- **CRA order holds.** Every idea opens concrete (drag/tap), moves to a picture,
  and meets the symbol last and small. No lesson opens on notation.
- **One idea per screen.** Paged, never a scroll or a wall of text; under ~50
  words per screen, read-aloud friendly.
- **Finger-sized targets.** Large hit areas and spacing; drag/tap/sketch over
  typing and tiny sliders.
- **Missions, not definitions.** Checks are "do it" tasks with picture-anchored
  options; never "what is…".
- **Celebrated.** Success is visibly rewarded (XP / unlock / burst); a game world
  fits.

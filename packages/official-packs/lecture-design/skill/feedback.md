# Move 5 — Feedback that says where-next, not just right/wrong

Feedback averages d ≈ 0.48 but varies wildly; **high-information feedback** (where
am I going / how am I going / where to next) beats bare correctness, and praise
does nothing (Hattie & Timperley 2007; Wisniewski, Zierer & Hattie 2020, 435
studies).

→ **Faraday:** write `<Quiz>` `hint`s as feed-forward ("re-run the sim with n=1
and watch the ends" — not "wrong"); the grounded tutor (the [`tutor`](../../tutor)
pack) is the richest feedback channel — Socratic, pointing back into the lesson.
